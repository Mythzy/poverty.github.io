Child Poverty - A Rising Issue In The UK

Child Poverty is an issue that affects 3.9 Million children in the UK. Thats 27% Of children in the UK, or eight in a classroom of 30.

Facts And Figures

49% Of children in lone-parent families are living in poverty.

75% Of children living in poverty live in a household where at least one adult works.

Children in Black and ethnic groups are more likely to be in poverty of which 46% of children of those groups are in poverty.
